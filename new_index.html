Welcome to myItinerary!

myIntinery allows you to search for locations and then add them to a printable Itinerary!

  1. Begin by entering a city below
  2. Create a title for your itinerary
  3. Use the green button to create a destination
  4. Then enter a keyword to search
  5. Choose your destination by clicking on the "Add Destination" button
  6. Let's begin!
